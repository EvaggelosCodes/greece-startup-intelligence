# The Founder (the lens every idea passes through)
*Read each run. Deliberately minimal — a tight profile would kill good options.*

- **Where:** Greece.
- **Capital:** near zero (under €500, ideally €0).
- **Team:** solo. Must be runnable by one person.
- **Urgency:** wants real revenue NOW, not someday. Prioritise first-30-days cash.
- **Open:** no fixed industry, skill, or location assumption. Keep the field wide.

**The filter:** for every surviving idea, answer one question honestly —
*"Would this specific person realistically start this on Monday with no money?"*
If no, it doesn't belong in the pool, however clever it is.

*If Evaggelos ever adds real constraints (a skill, a city, a contact, money tried),*
*record them here as one-liners — but never invent constraints he didn't give.*

# Greece Seasonal Engine
*Greece runs on seasons more than almost any EU economy. Every TIMING score must*
*be read against the CURRENT month. Check today's date, find the row, adjust.*

---

| Month | Economic rhythm | What's hot to start NOW |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------------|
| Jan | Dead season, post-holiday cash crunch. ESPA calls often open Q1. | Off-season services, tax/admin help, ESPA application help |
| Feb | Dead. Carnival (Apokries) builds late month. | Carnival/events, indoor experiences |
| Mar | Shoulder begins. Season-prep hiring starts. Indep. Day (25th) travel. | Tourism-supplier services, staffing, island prep |
| Apr | **Greek Easter** (2026: Apr 12) = biggest domestic travel + food spend. Islands wake. | Easter logistics, food, short-term rental setup, season staffing |
| May | Shoulder ramps hard. Weddings start. Pre-summer hiring peak. | Wedding services, tourism onboarding, rental prep |
| Jun | Summer in full swing. Graduations. Diaspora arrivals begin. | Tourist-facing services, diaspora services, experiences |
| Jul | **Peak tourism.** Summer sales (εκπτώσεις) mid-month. | Anything tourist-facing, instant-cash services |
| Aug | Peak tourism BUT Greece "closes" mid-Aug (15th). Locals away, B2B dead, Athens empties. | Tourist-only plays. Avoid B2B/local launches |
| Sep | Best shoulder month for many. Schools reopen, back-to-business. Diaspora leaves. | Back-to-business services, B2B relaunch, education |
| Oct | Shoulder tail. Business planning season. ESPA autumn calls. | B2B, planning/consulting, ESPA help, education |
| Nov | Dead season starts. **Black Friday is huge in Greece.** | E-commerce, Black Friday plays, off-season service gap |
| Dec | Holidays: Christmas spend + diaspora returns for holidays. Year-end. | Gifting, diaspora/holiday services, year-end admin |

---

**Two-market rule:** tourism = supply-constrained in summer, demand-constrained in
winter. A summer-only idea scores a -0.5 penalty unless it has an off-season version.

**Right now (update if stale):** late June 2026 — peak summer ramping, diaspora
arriving, B2B sluggish until September. Favour tourist- and diaspora-facing, fast-cash
ideas. Park anything needing Greek businesses to buy until Sept.

### STR Compliance Concierge - Score: 6.9/10
**What it is:** Fixed-fee audit and coordination service that helps small STR/Airbnb owners line up 2026 registration, safety, insurance and paperwork requirements through the right specialists.
**Why Greece specifically:** Greek STR regulation creates a sudden mandatory compliance window for thousands of owners; fines can start around EUR5,000 while many owners are still subscale and self-managing.
**How to start tomorrow:** (1) Build a plain-Greek 10-point STR compliance audit/checklist. (2) Pitch 20 accountants, property managers, electricians, insurers and fire-safety suppliers who already educate owners: "send me confused owner clients; I organize the checklist and route specialist work back to you." (3) Charge a flat low fee per property, then coordinate specialists only when needed.
**Revenue model:** Flat audit fee EUR79-EUR99/property, setup coordination EUR120-EUR180/property, plus optional annual stay-compliant check and referral commissions.
**Realistic Month 1 revenue:** EUR300-EUR1,200 if one referral channel opens or a handful of owners convert off summer urgency.
**Biggest risk:** Paid-intent search now routes owners not only to guides, managers, accountants, legal advisors, insurers and specialists, but also to management-assignment sheets and end-to-end consultant/engineer bundles, so the solo coordinator may remain optional unless direct outreach or referral tests convert.
**Competitor landscape:** Existing firms and specialist providers mean the gap is not blank-market; even compliance-check and management language is already visible across STR operators, consultants and specialist pages, so the credible wedge is only a cheaper owner-centric checklist plus specialist routing.
**Timing signal:** July peak tourism and EUR5,000+ downside keep the pain real, but public search is exhausted; only a EUR49-EUR99 checklist outreach or referral test can raise confidence now.

### Webinar-to-Course Packaging Sprint - Score: 6.5/10
**What it is:** Fixed-fee service that turns an existing Greek Zoom seminar, workshop or expert presentation into a sellable replay/course mini-funnel for people too small for platforms/agencies: landing page copy, checkout setup guidance, email sequence, replay packaging, worksheet and 10 social posts.
**Why Greece specifically:** Greek professionals already sell online seminars, certificates and Zoom trainings, and Greek course platforms/tooling exist; many experts have content but not a clean repeatable productized funnel.
**How to start tomorrow:** (1) Find 30 Greek professionals already advertising live Zoom seminars or paid workshops. (2) Send a short audit: "you have the seminar, but no replay product/follow-up funnel." (3) Offer a EUR149 pilot sprint that packages one existing seminar into a replay offer using their current platform or a no-code checkout.
**Revenue model:** EUR149 pilot packaging sprint, EUR300-EUR500 full funnel build, optional monthly email/content support.
**Realistic Month 1 revenue:** EUR300-EUR1,200 if 2-4 seminar sellers convert.
**Biggest risk:** Platforms, templates, funnel tools, webinar-ops providers and marketing agencies may already satisfy serious course creators, so the wedge only works for professionals who already sell expertise but have under-packaged seminar assets and no appetite for a big system.
**Competitor landscape:** LearnWorlds/eCourses/LMS tools cover infrastructure; ClickFunnels/GoHighLevel/templates cover funnels; digital agencies and webinar-ops providers cover bigger execution. The opening is a small one-off execution sprint for experts who do not want a big agency or new platform.
